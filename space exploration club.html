NASA's missions have revolutionized space exploration and science. **Apollo 11** achieved the first Moon landing, while the **Hubble Telescope** revealed the universe's structure. **Voyager 1 and 2** explored the outer planets and entered interstellar space. **Mars Rovers** found evidence of water, and the **ISS** fosters global collaboration in space research. The **James Webb Telescope** uncovers the early universe, and the **Artemis Program** aims to return humans to the Moon. **New Horizons** explored Pluto, **Cassini** revealed Saturn's mysteries, and **Pioneer missions** paved the way for outer solar system exploration.
